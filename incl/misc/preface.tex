This project is made by the group mattekC.2-5, on the first semester of the Mathematics-technology education on Aalborg University. When reading this project, the reader must know atleast Mathematics A level. The purpose of this project is to use the basic theory of differential equations, circuit theory, complex numbers and the laplace transformation to set up a mathematic model of a charge and discharge in a RC-circuit and for HPF/LPF.\\\\
The group would like to thank the supervisors John-Josef Leth and Kristian Hessellund.
