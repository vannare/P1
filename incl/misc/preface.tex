This project is written by the group Mattek C.2-5 on the 1st semester of Mathematics-Technology at Aalborg University. In this project, the Vancouver method is used when referencing. In this report, Leibniz notation is used for derivatives. Readers of this project are assumed to have knowledge equal to at least A-level Mathematics.\\\\
