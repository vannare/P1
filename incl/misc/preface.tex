This project is completed by the group mattek1c2-5 on the 1st semester of Mathematics-Technology at Aalborg University. The purpose of this project is to construct a mathematical model of the charging and discharging in an RC circuit, and for high-pass/low-pass filters in signal processing. This can be done using differential equations, circuit theory, complex numbers, and the Laplace transform. Readers of this project are assumed to have knowledge equal to at least A-level Mathematics.\\\\
The group would like to thank supervisors John-Josef Leth and Kristian Hessellund for their guidance and feedback.
